About Vishvash

Welcome to Vishvash Beauty Parlour, where we believe that every person deserves to feel beautiful and confident. Our team of licensed professionals is dedicated to providing you with personalized service and the latest techniques and products in the beauty industry.

Our History

Founded in 2005 by Ms. kiran devji bhai vala, Vishvash Beauty Parlour began as a small business with just two employees. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most trusted and respected beauty parlours in the city, serving hundreds of clients each month.

Our Philosophy

At Vishvash, our goal is to help you look and feel your best. We believe that beauty comes from within, and that each person has their own unique style and personality. Our team will work with you to create a customized beauty plan that suits your individual needs and preferences. Whether you want to relax and pamper yourself, or you need a quick touch-up before a big event, we've got you covered.

Our Services

From hair care and skincare to makeup and massages, Vishvash offers a full range of beauty services for women and men of all ages. Our talented techniciansuse only the highest quality products and equipment to ensure that you get the best possible results.

Some of our most popular services include:

  • Haircuts and styling
  • Facials and skincare treatments
  • Manicures and pedicures
  • Makeup application and lessons
  • Massage therapy
